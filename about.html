About

[G4 Pathology] Pty Limited (ACN 608 029 732) is a specialist provider of genomic pathology services and a whole owned subsidiary of the Garvan Institute of Medical Research.

[G4 Pathology] uses specialised whole genome sequencing (WGS) technology for diagnostic screening of genetic disorders. [G4 Pathology] is accredited by NATA for Medical Testing and complies with AS ISO 15189, in accordance with Australian pathology accreditation requirements.
